Picking Mulberries: Double Ninth

Text: Life is finite, yet heaven endures; year after year comes the Double Ninth. Today again is the Double Ninth, and the yellow blossoms on the battlefield are especially fragrant. Each year the autumn wind blows with force; it is unlike the light of spring, yet surpasses spring, as the vast river-and-sky stretch beneath ten thousand miles of frost.
