Ascending Mount Lu

Text: A lone mountain rises beside the great river; ascending through four hundred winding turns into lush greenery. With a cool eye I look out upon the world across the seas; warm winds and rain sweep across the river sky. Clouds over the nine branches of the river float past the Yellow Crane, while waves below the three Wu regions stir white mist. Where has Magistrate Tao gone? Might one still till the fields within Peach Blossom Spring?
