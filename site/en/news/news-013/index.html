University hosts exhibition of Zhang Mantang's copies of Chairman Mao calligraphy

On 4 January 2008, a report exhibition of Zhang Mantang's copies of Chairman Mao's calligraphy was held in the university art building exhibition hall, organised by local education, literary and youth bodies in Binzhou. The report said 114 Mao-style calligraphy works, copied by Zhang over many years, were shown at the exhibition.
