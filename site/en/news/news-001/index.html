Zhang Mantang receives China-Japan cultural exchange title

In July 2009, Zhang Mantang took part in the Tokyo China Calligraphy and Fine Arts Exhibition, where he won a gold award and was also given the title of China-Japan Cultural Exchange Friendship Envoy.
