Qingdao Museum stages exhibition of Mao Zedong poetry calligraphy treasures

According to information released by Qingdao Museum, the Exhibition of Mao Zedong Poetry Calligraphy Treasures opened on 1 July 2011 and ran until 6 July. The show presented 90 selected works of Mao Zedong poetry calligraphy and also displayed Mao-style handwritten copies created by Wang Jiadou and Zhang Mantang.
