Dialogue Binzhou profiles calligrapher Zhang Mantang

A feature on Dialogue Binzhou profiled Mao-style calligrapher Zhang Mantang, focusing on his artistic practice, charity work, efforts to bring calligraphy into businesses and his wider creative pursuits. The report also said his work had been collected by the Great Hall of the People and that several calligraphic works based on quotations by Xi Jinping had been used for stamp issues in Eurasian countries.
